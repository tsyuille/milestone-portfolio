About Me

I am passionate about creating amazing stuff.

I am a web designer born and raised (and currently based in) central Virginia. I transitioned into tech after a successful (but unfulfilling) career in accounting and finance. As a creative with a budding interest in designing and building websites, I began taking courses in line with my interests and working with clients on projects. I pay close attention to details, and make sure that the final product makes myself and my clients proud. I'm serious about my work, but I don't take myself too seriously. I frequent museums. Good food is my love language. I am a HUGE fan of music. So if we’re ever playing a game involving lyrics or music trivia, you’re gonna want me on your team.

Can't wait to work and create with you!
